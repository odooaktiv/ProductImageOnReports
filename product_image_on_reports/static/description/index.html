Product Image on Sales Invoice Purchase Report

In the default, Odoo functionality product image wasn’t displayed on sales/invoice/purchase reports. This module is helpful in displaying the product image on sales order report, invoice report, and purchase report.

Prerequisites before Installation

No prerequisites needed.

Configurations

No configurations.

Once you install this module and user places an order, you can see the product image on your Sale Order Report.

Once having placed your order, you can view the product image on your order’s invoice report too.

For the buyers, when purchase order report is generated, they can view the product image on the report other than description, date of order placed, and quantity information.
